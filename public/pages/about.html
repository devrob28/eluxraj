Our Story

Founded by Alternative Asset Veterans

ELUXRAJ was founded in 2024 by a team of former hedge fund managers, AI researchers, and alternative asset specialists who recognized a critical gap in the market.

While traditional investment platforms focused on stocks and bonds, the most sophisticated wealth builders were quietly accumulating alternative assets: rare collectibles, precious metals, private equity, and unique opportunities invisible to conventional markets.

We built ELUXRAJ to level the playing field—bringing institutional-grade alternative asset intelligence to ambitious investors worldwide.

Today, we serve clients managing over $2.4 billion in alternative assets, delivering signals that have outperformed traditional benchmarks by an average of 12.7% annually.
